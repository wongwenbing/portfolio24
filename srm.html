Statistical Report on Impacts of Technology

This report examines technology.

Project Scope

The project's scope is to conduct a thorough analysis of the multifaceted impacts of technology across diverse domains. It encompasses economic, societal, and environmental dimensions, aiming to provide a comprehensive understanding of how technology influences various sectors. The exploration extends beyond merely quantifying impacts, also seeking to uncover patterns, correlations, and trends associated with technological advancements.

Our Work Process

Over a span of 2 months, our extensive work process involved meticulous coordination and planning.

Examining Hypothesis

We had to examine the impacts of technology in 5 different areas: academic, mental health, income, learning effectiveness, budgets. As such, a lot of research was done into crafting the sub-hypothesis and the studies.

Conducting Surveys

Based on the research and sub-hypothesis, we had to craft questions for our hypothesis. Some of our considerations were:

  • Ensuring that our results do not skew towards a certain group as much as possible
  • Ensuring that our survey have a good mix of open-ended and closed-ended questions
  • Ensuring that the survey data obtained would be fruitful for analysis

Conducting Staristical Analysis

Undertaking statistical analysis involves a thorough examination of the survey data collected. Through a meticulous exploration of the gathered information, we have employed various statistical methods and tools to analyze patterns, trends, and correlations within the dataset. The aim is to uncover key findings that contribute to a comprehensive understanding of the underlying factors and dynamics shaping the research.

Writing Report

Following the completion of the statistical analysis, the next crucial step is the articulation of our findings through a detailed and comprehensive report. This involves synthesising the results into a coherent narrative, highlighting significant observations, and drawing insightful conclusions. The report will serve as documentation of the statistical analysis process, providing valuable information and recommendations based on the survey data. The goal is to present a well-structured and informative document that effectively communicates the outcomes of our research endeavour.

Outcome
