Hotel Website

Through the use of web development tools, we successfully developed a full-fledged front-end website for San Marimo Resorts.

Project Scope

For our project scope, we are tasked with constructing a hotel website utilizing HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Our focus encompasses not only the foundational development but also ensuring a responsive design for an optimal user experience across various devices. Our Work Process

Developing Individual Wireframe

Developing individual wireframes is a critical part of the design process, providing a skeletal framework for digital interfaces. Designers focus on organizing key elements for optimal usability and navigation without getting into detailed visual elements. Wireframes serve as a foundation for collaborative discussions, aligning design goals and refining user experience before proceeding to more polished design stages.

Developing Individual Prototype and Pitching to Lecturers

We had to develop our indivudal prototype to the lecturers to seek their ideas. We were also taught on how do we pitch the ideas which I found very useful for me. We had to present our ideas, how we were going to achieve a good user experience through the different concepts of usability, and how we had planned to implement it.
Based on the feedback from my lecturers, I was able to work on them and further modify my website to prepare for the final integration.

Integrating the final website together as a group

I was responsible for the final website merging. This involved merging, ensuring that the styles are similar, looking like exact replicas of each other but with different content.
